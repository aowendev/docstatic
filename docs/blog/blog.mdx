# Create a Blog article

## Create a blog article from the CMS

1. From the **TinaCMS** menu, under **Collections**, click **Blog Articles**.
2. Click **Add File**.
3. Enter a **Title**.
4. Enter a **Description**.
5. Add any **Tags**.
6. Enter the article text.
7. When you are done, click **Save**.

To publish the article immediately, change the **Document Status** to **Published**.

Docusaurus creates a **page for each blog post**, but also a **blog index page**, a **tag system**, an **RSS** feed...
